`LittlestTokyo.glb` — “Littlest Tokyo” by Glen Fox.

License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

Source: https://threejs.org/examples/#webgl_animation_keyframes

The model is used as the animated civic-city centerpiece of MANDAT//ZERO. The surrounding monoliths, orbital network, particles, grid, lighting, shaders, and post-processing are generated at runtime with Three.js.
